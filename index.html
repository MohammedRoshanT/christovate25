WHAT IS CHRISTOVATE?

Christovate is an innovation-focused makethon for school students (Grades 8–12) and college students. Participants identify real-world problems and develop creative, tech-driven, open-source solutions. Organized by Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda, Kerala, in association with FOSS, CSI Student Chapter, ICFOSS, IEEE, and IETE, the event promotes mentorship, collaboration, and hands-on prototyping to inspire future changemakers.

OUR AIM

To inspire students to move beyond textbooks, explore practical solutions and develop critical thinking, teamwork and presentation skills through ideation, pitching, mentoring, prototyping.
